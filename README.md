## Destination Details
This page will dedicately describe a destination to the tourist get his primary idea about the place
This section should be as direct as it could for tourist to get information
It should not confuse user and get a over all views of the place so that tourist can plan his trip accordingly


## 1. Hero Section

**Purpose:** Give an instant emotional & visual impression.
* **Image Slider (3–5 images)**
  High-quality images in a simple slider with arrows + bullets.
  Images should showcase: main attraction, vibe, scenery, activities.
* **Destination Name + Location**
  Include city, region, and country.
* **Essential Tags (Pills)** (optional, helps quick scanning)
  Examples: *Nature*, *Historic*, *Nightlife*, *Budget-Friendly*, *Beach*.


## 2. Quick Overview

**Purpose:** One paragraph that gives the essence in 10–15 seconds.

* 2–3 line short description
  Should answer:

  * Why visit this place?
  * What makes it unique?
  * What type of tourist will enjoy it?

---

## 3. Key Highlights

**Purpose:** Provide fast, skimmable facts.

Format: bullet points or icons
Examples:

* Best time to visit
* Suitable for: solo, couples, families, adventurers
* Popular for: beaches / nightlife / culture / nature / food
* Cost level: low / medium / high
* Average duration of stay: 1 day / 2–3 days / a week

---

## 4. Stay Options

Split into two sections for clarity:

### A. Types of Stay

Show categories with a quick description:

* Hostels
* Hotels
* Resorts
* Guesthouses
* Homestays (optional)

Each category can show approximate price ranges.

### B. Suggested Stays

Top 5–10 places based on rating, distance, and pricing:

* Name
* Price range
* Rating
* Distance from main attraction
* Button: **View Details** / **Book** (if integrated)

---

## 5. Must-See Spots

Show all nearby attractions with quick info cards.

Each includes:

* Name
* Distance from center
* Short 1-line why it’s special
* Tag (e.g., *Nature*, *Historic*, *Photo spot*)

(Users exploring destinations often want this section first.)

---

## 6. Transportation

Simple and clear.

* **How to reach there**
  (Airport, train, bus availability, travel time)
* **Local transport options:**

  * Bus
  * Rickshaw / tuk-tuk / taxi
  * Rental scooters or cars
  * Ride-sharing availability
* **Price range:**
  Example: “Tuk-tuk: 100–300 BDT for short distances”

Optional: show a cost index (low / medium / high).

---

## 7. Local Cuisine

Short + visual if possible.

* Signature dishes
* What they're famous for
* Must-try restaurants (3–5 names)

Keep it simple: tourists browse this fast.

---

## 8. Popular Activities

Include:

* Adventure activities
* Cultural experiences
* Seasonal events
* Tours and guides
* Price ranges

Example:

* Boat ride — 300–600 BDT
* Hiking trail — free
* Local guided tour — 1000–2000 BDT

---

## 9. Best Time to Visit

* Weather patterns
* Peak vs off-season
* Festival periods

This helps planning instantly.

---

## 10. Safety & Tips

Keep this short but valuable.

* Local customs
* Common scams to avoid (without exaggeration)
* Dress expectations (if relevant)
* Emergency contacts (optional)

---

## 11. Additional Information

Fast facts:

* Languages commonly used
* Payment methods (cash/card/mobile money)
* Connectivity availability (WiFi, SIM cards)
* Cultural norms
* Weather expectations
* Any permits or tickets needed
